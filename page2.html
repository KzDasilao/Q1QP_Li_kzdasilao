Information

This website is our reasearch about Customer's Product Choices. Understanding the demands of customers is critical for business. Our data set contains information about clients and the things they have purchased. This data set was obtained from the website Kaggle and released by Mohammad Talib. Our data collection consists of a sample of 1000 clients. We chose this topic because we want to know which product options customers prefer. Furthermore, we want to see how far these clients are willing to go. This is intended to assist sales personnel in determining which products consumers like. We shall demonstrate two qualitative and quantitative factors. Gender and product category would be our qualitative variables. While the age and total amount paid would be our quantitative variables.
