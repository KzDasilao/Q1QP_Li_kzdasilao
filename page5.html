Conclusion

According to the data we collected, women outnumber males when it comes to buying. The findings show that clothing was chosen as the most favored product by customers in the product category. According to Forbes (2013), women dominate the shopping industry because "women have primary caregiving responsibilities," which causes "women to find themselves buying on behalf of everyone else in their lives." It is understandable why clothing was chosen as the highest in the product category because clothing is a necessity for us. According to Fibre2Fashion (2006), clothing is necessary "to protect it against adverse climate conditions." Apart from being a need, it enhances our beauty and boosts our self-esteem. This is relevant to businesses. Salespeople can use this as a tool to observe consumer preferences for what they would buy or products in high demand. This information can be used to forecast future product preferences. This can assist firms in estimating product demand, managing inventories, and making strategic decisions to efficiently meet their consumers' requests.
